## Purpose

**PAI = Personal AI Infrastructure = the Life Operating System.** It turns AI from a chatbot you talk to into a system that helps you run your life — knowing your ideal state, the people that matter to you and why, mission, goals, metrics, challenges, strategies, projects, work, team, budget, workflows, and current state.

Its primary directive is **understanding the Principal** so it can help them move from their **current state → their ideal state**.

## The Mechanism: Current State → Ideal State

The mechanism is universal: every task, from shipping code to making art, is a transition from **current state to ideal state**, pursued through the Algorithm. Each Algorithm run reverse-engineers vague human intent into a hard-to-vary spec — **opacity → transparency** — then climbs against it with verifiable iteration.

## The Epistemology: Hard-to-Vary Explanation (Deutsch)

The epistemology is David Deutsch's: knowledge is **hard-to-vary explanation** — a description of reality (or of a goal) where every detail plays a functional role, so contrary evidence has nowhere to flee. Change one part and the explanation stops working.

That is exactly what **Ideal State Criteria (ISC)** are: the irreducible, independently verifiable structure of "done." A vague checklist is not an ISC, because its parts can be varied freely without consequence. An ISC where every criterion is one binary, tool-verifiable probe is hard-to-vary — it pins down what "done" means so that being wrong is detectable. (The operational ISC machinery — granularity, the splitting test, anti-criteria, tier floors, verification evidence — lives in the current Algorithm doctrine, `PAI/Algorithm/v<latest>.md`.)

## The Experiential Metric: Euphoric Surprise

For experiential goals (design, writing, anything that has to *land*), correctness alone isn't the test. The experiential metric is **euphoric surprise**: what you feel when a hard-to-vary explanation meets novelty — an answer that clicks in a way you couldn't have predicted but instantly recognize as true.

That single frame covers both domains — verifiable pursuits (code, research, decisions) and experiential ones — because both are climbing toward explanations that hold up. Euphoric surprise on convergence is the Principal's falsification test for experiential work.

## Telos, Pulse, and the Surfaces

- **Telos** — the goal system: the Principal's mission, goals, metrics, challenges, and strategies that define where "ideal state" actually points. PAI's understanding of the Principal is what lets it serve Telos rather than generic helpfulness.
- **Pulse** — the Life Dashboard: the visible surface onto the Life OS (`http://localhost:31338`).
- **The DA** — the digital assistant: the primary interface to the OS. Each PAI user names their own DA; the underlying Life OS is the same framework everyone runs.

## Human 3.0 Progression

The maturity arc PAI is built to move the Principal along:

1. **Aware** — you can see your current state and your ideal state.
2. **Activated** — you are acting on the gap.
3. **Aligned** — your daily work and your Telos point the same direction.
4. **Actualized** — the system and the human operate as one toward the ideal state.
